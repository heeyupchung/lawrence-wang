LAWRENCE WANG is an aspiring food writer based in Brooklyn, New York, and born and raised in Rowland Heights, California. He recently graduated from New York University and worked in Revenue Operations before pivoting to the food world. His passion is telling people's stories through food, which you can read about on his blog, Your Round Table. In his spare time he develops recipes, plays music, plays games, and does the daily Wordle online (along with its many variations).
